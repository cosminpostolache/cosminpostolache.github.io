Cosmin Postolache · Patru cântece

A collection of songs in older styles: ballad, folk, chanson and latin.

A study in altering the timbre of the piano through electronic FX more familiar to guitarists and synth players, such as delay and reverb. Conceived to be played live with the aid of a pedalboard, this study evolves into a quasi-polyphonic texture, reminiscent of the old form known as invention .
